Billing & Payments Help

Comprehensive guide to payment methods, rental fees, security deposits, and billing policies

Payment Information

Accepted Payment Methods

We offer multiple secure payment options to ensure a seamless rental experience. All transactions are processed through encrypted, PCI-compliant systems.

CD

Credit & Debit Cards

Visa, MasterCard, American Express accepted. 3D Secure authentication required for online payments.

BT

Bank Transfer

Direct EFT payments with instant verification. Use your online banking platform for secure transfers.

CS

Cash Payments

Available for in-store collections only. Exact change preferred, receipts provided for all transactions.

Our transparent pricing ensures you know exactly what you're paying for. All prices include VAT.

How are rental fees calculated?

Rental fees are determined by several factors including:

  • Dress Category: Premium designer dresses command higher rates
  • Rental Duration: Longer rentals offer better daily rates
  • Seasonal Demand: Peak season (December, June) may have premium pricing
  • Dress Condition: New arrivals and exclusive pieces may have special rates
What's included in the rental fee?

Your comprehensive rental package includes:

  • Professional dry cleaning and steam pressing
  • Minor alterations (hemming, taking in/out up to one size)
  • Protective garment bag for transportation
  • Damage insurance covering accidental stains and minor repairs
  • 24/7 style consultation during your rental period
  • Emergency repair kit for minor on-site fixes

Security Deposit Policy

Security deposits protect both you and our collection. They're fully refundable when dresses are returned in original condition.

How much is the security deposit?

Security deposits are calculated as follows:

  • Standard Dresses: R500 deposit
  • Premium Designer: R1,000 deposit
  • Luxury Collection: R2,000 deposit
  • Bridal & Special Occasion: R2,500 deposit

The exact deposit amount is clearly displayed during checkout and confirmed in your rental agreement.

Deposit refund process and timeline?

Our streamlined refund process ensures you receive your deposit promptly:

  • Inspection: Dresses undergo quality inspection within 24 hours of return
  • Processing: Refunds are processed within 3 business days post-inspection
  • Bank Transfer: 3-5 business days for bank processing
  • Card Refunds: 5-7 business days depending on your bank

You'll receive email confirmation at each stage of the refund process.

What affects deposit refund eligibility?

Deposit deductions may apply for the following:

  • Stains: Red wine, makeup, or permanent stains that require specialized cleaning
  • Damage: Tears, holes, broken zippers, or missing buttons
  • Alterations: Unauthorized alterations or modifications
  • Missing Items: Belts, sashes, or accessories not returned
  • Late Returns: Returns after 6pm on due date without prior arrangement
  • Smoke/Odor: Cigarette smoke or strong perfumes requiring ozone treatment

Billing & Account Management

Clear policies and procedures for all billing-related matters.

Payment requirements and timing?

To secure your reservation, we require:

  • Full Payment: Rental fee + security deposit due at booking
  • Confirmation: Booking confirmed immediately upon payment
  • Last-Minute Bookings: Same-day bookings require payment within 2 hours
  • Corporate Accounts: Net 30 terms available for approved business accounts
Invoices and documentation?

We provide comprehensive documentation:

  • Digital Invoice: Automatically emailed upon payment
  • Rental Agreement: Detailed terms and conditions
  • Receipts: Available for all transactions
  • Tax Documentation: VAT invoices for business claims
  • Re-issuing: Previous invoices available in your account dashboard
Cancellation and refund policies?

Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to all parties:

  • 7+ Days Notice: Full refund of rental fee
  • 48-168 Hours Notice: 50% refund of rental fee
  • Under 48 Hours: 25% refund of rental fee
  • Same Day Cancellation: Rental fee retained, security deposit refunded
  • No Show: Full rental fee retained

Security deposits are always fully refundable for cancellations.

Need immediate billing assistance? Contact our dedicated billing team at +27 74 497 8123 or email billing@ozyde.com for prompt resolution of payment-related inquiries.
