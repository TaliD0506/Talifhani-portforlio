Delivery Process & Timeline

How does the delivery process work?

Our streamlined delivery process ensures your dress arrives perfectly:

  • Order Confirmation: Immediately after payment
  • Quality Check: Dress inspected and prepared within 4 hours
  • Dispatch: Assigned to courier with tracking information sent
  • In Transit: Real-time tracking updates via SMS and email
  • Delivery: Signature required, contactless option available
  • Confirmation: Delivery photo and digital receipt provided

Returns & Collection Process

How do returns and collections work?

Our convenient return process makes it easy:

  • Scheduled Collection: We collect the dress from your specified address
  • Return Packaging: Use the provided garment bag and box
  • Collection Time: Same 2-hour windows as delivery
  • No Additional Cost: Returns included in original delivery fee
  • Inspection: Quick visual inspection at collection
  • Receipt: Digital collection confirmation provided
Delivery Support: For urgent delivery inquiries or to speak with our courier coordination team, call +27 74 497 8123 or message us on WhatsApp for real-time assistance.
