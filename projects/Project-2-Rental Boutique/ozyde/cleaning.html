Cleaning & Care Guide

Proper care ensures our luxury dresses maintain their beauty and quality. Follow these guidelines to keep your rental in perfect condition.

Fabric-Specific Care Instructions

S Silk & Satin

✓
Dry clean only or hand wash gently in cold water
✓
Iron on low heat with a cloth barrier
✗
Avoid wringing or twisting the fabric
✗
Never use bleach or harsh detergents

L Lace & Embroidery

✓
Hand wash in cold water with mild detergent
✓
Lay flat to dry to maintain shape
✗
Avoid machine washing or drying
✗
Don't rub or scrub delicate areas

C Cotton & Linen

✓
Machine wash cold on gentle cycle
✓
Iron while slightly damp for best results
✗
Avoid high heat in dryer
✗
Don't use fabric softener on linen

B Beaded & Sequined

✓
Spot clean only with damp cloth
✓
Store flat or hang with padding
✗
Never machine wash or dry clean
✗
Avoid folding to prevent damage

⚠ Emergency Care Tips

For spills and accidents during your rental:

  • Blot (don't rub) spills immediately with a clean, dry cloth
  • For wine spills, sprinkle with salt and let sit before brushing off
  • For oil-based stains, use cornstarch to absorb before cleaning
  • Contact us immediately for serious stains - we're here to help!

Common Stain Removal Guide

Red Wine

Blot immediately, then pour white wine or club soda on the stain. Rinse with cold water and apply salt. Let sit before brushing off.

Makeup

Use a makeup remover wipe or mild dish soap with cold water. Gently dab until stain lifts. Avoid rubbing.

Food & Grease

Scrape off excess, then apply cornstarch or talcum powder to absorb oil. Brush off after 15 minutes before washing.

Grass

Apply rubbing alcohol or white vinegar to the stain, then rinse with cold water. Wash as usual.

Blood

Soak in cold water with salt or hydrogen peroxide. Never use hot water as it sets the stain.

Ink

Apply hairspray or rubbing alcohol to the stain, then blot with a clean cloth. Repeat until ink transfers to cloth.

Important Notes

  • Always test cleaning solutions on an inconspicuous area first
  • For delicate fabrics, consult with us before attempting stain removal
  • Never use bleach on colored fabrics
  • When in doubt, professional cleaning is always the safest option

Proper Storage Guidelines

Correct storage preserves the quality and longevity of our luxury dresses between wears.

♻

Between Events

Hang dresses on padded hangers in a cool, dry place. Allow fabrics to breathe between wears.

Long-term Storage

Use breathable garment bags. Avoid plastic which can trap moisture and cause yellowing.

Folding Tips

Fold along seams to prevent creasing. Use acid-free tissue paper to cushion folds.

Storage Don'ts

  • Never store dresses in direct sunlight - causes fading
  • Avoid damp areas - promotes mold and mildew
  • Don't overcrowd in closet - can cause permanent wrinkles
  • Avoid scented sachets directly on fabric - can cause staining
