How Ozyde Works

Renting luxury dresses has never been easier. Follow these simple steps to find, book, and wear your perfect dress for any occasion.

1

Browse & Select

Explore our curated collection of luxury dresses for weddings, matric dances, birthdays, and special events. Use our filters to find dresses by size, color, style, or occasion.

Each dress listing includes multiple photos, detailed descriptions, available sizes, and rental pricing. Save your favorites to easily compare and decide.

[Browse Collection Image]
2

Book Your Dates

Select your preferred rental dates and complete your booking. Our calendar shows real-time availability to ensure your dress will be ready when you need it.

Choose between our standard 4-day rental or extended options for longer events. We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance for popular styles and sizes.

[Booking Calendar Image]
3

Try On & Confirm

Schedule a complimentary fitting session at our boutique or request our at-home try-on service (available in select areas). Our stylists will help ensure the perfect fit.

If you need alterations, we offer minor adjustments to make sure your dress fits flawlessly. Final confirmation is required 7 days before your rental period begins.

[Fitting Session Image]
4

Receive & Wear

Your dress arrives carefully packaged with a garment bag, care instructions, and any accessories you've selected. We offer nationwide delivery or boutique pickup.

Wear your Ozyde dress with confidence, knowing you're wearing a professionally cleaned, high-quality garment that will make you stand out at your event.

[Dress Delivery Image]
5

Return & Repeat

After your event, simply return the dress using our prepaid shipping label or drop it off at our boutique. We handle all cleaning and maintenance.

Leave a review and earn loyalty points for your next rental. Many of our members rent for multiple events throughout the year!

[Return Process Image]
Q

Quality Guaranteed

Every dress is professionally cleaned, inspected, and maintained to ensure it looks impeccable for your special occasion.

D

Free Delivery

We offer complimentary delivery and returns in major metropolitan areas. Nationwide shipping available at competitive rates.

P

Flexible Payment

Pay only for the time you need. Choose from multiple rental durations and payment options to suit your budget.

S

Stylist Support

Our expert stylists are available to help you choose the perfect dress and ensure it fits flawlessly for your event.
