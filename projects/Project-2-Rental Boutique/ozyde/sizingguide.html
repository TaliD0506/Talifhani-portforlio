1 How to Measure Your Bust

[Illustration: Bust measurement]

Wrap the measuring tape around the fullest part of your bust, keeping the tape parallel to the floor. Make sure the tape is snug but not tight.

2 How to Measure Your Waist

[Illustration: Waist measurement]

Find your natural waistline (the narrowest part of your torso) and wrap the measuring tape around it. Keep the tape parallel to the floor.

3 How to Measure Your Hips

[Illustration: Hip measurement]

Stand with your feet together and wrap the measuring tape around the fullest part of your hips and buttocks. Keep the tape parallel to the floor.

Tips for Finding Your Perfect Fit

1
Use a flexible tape measure

For accurate measurements, use a soft, flexible measuring tape rather than a rigid ruler.

2
Wear appropriate undergarments

Measure while wearing the undergarments you plan to wear with the dress for the most accurate fit.

3
Stand naturally

Avoid sucking in your stomach or standing unnaturally straight while taking measurements.

4
Get help if needed

For the most accurate measurements, have someone assist you, especially for hard-to-reach areas.
